About

55 Peterborough Ave.

Our 3-storey house is located in the heart of Corso Italia, a historic Italian community. The neighbourhood has a distinct cultural flavour with a series of traditional Italian restaurants, bakeries, gelaterias and an Italian Catholic church located along several blocks of Saint Clair West avenue. Largest Italian street festival in Toronto, Corso Italia Festival is held here every July.

House is conveniently located on a quiet street steps away from Toronto's two major arteries - Dufferin st. and St.Clair Avenue. We have a lovely garden, summer veranda and a back yard with options of summer barbecue.
